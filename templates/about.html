A little bit about me

Hi! My name is Shivam. I am an aspiring data scientist. Ok, I might be aspiring to become a data scientist now but that was not the original plan. I am a mechanical engineering graduate by the way. The story of me wanting to become a data scientist started with the learning of python programming language. I started learning python just because my friend told me that it is interesting. Being a mechanical engineering student, I had no exposure to programming before this. Around the same time while watching some of YouTube videos, I came to know that there is something called data science or machine learning to be exact. I started to explore this field after this. The thing is I very much enjoyed graphs and visualization from the childhood. Also growing up mathematics was my favorite subject. And while exploring, I was again finding those interesting things in the field of data science. Of course, mechanical engineering topic was interesting but this was more interesting. So, I started learning simple libraries like NumPy, Pandas and matplotlib. My interest in the field was increasing day by day that led to learning other thing like SQL, flask, web scrapping, tableau, statistics, machine learning and then finally decided to build my career in this the same field.

Besides the programming related things, I also enjoy my hobbies. I like to play chess, badminton with my friends and cousins. Also, roaming the city on my bicycle with my friends is one of the things I frequently enjoy. I also play racing games a lot on my personal computer. Sometimes I read the historical novels too. I have not travelled a lot yet but going forward planning to go to lot of treks and camps.
